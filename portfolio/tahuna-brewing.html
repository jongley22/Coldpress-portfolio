— Our Story

Brewed where
the water's cold.

Tāhuna is the Māori name for Queenstown — a shallow bay, a gathering place. We started brewing here in 2019 because we believed the South needed a beer that matched it. Not a brand designed by committee. A brewery with a point of view.

Every batch is brewed with water from the Shotover catchment, unfiltered and cold. We don't cut corners on ingredients and we don't apologise for our prices. The Remarkables are right there. The bar is high by default.

You'll find us on tap from Invercargill to Auckland. If you're in Queenstown, come in and meet us properly.
