Raglan, Waikato — 2021

Started in
a van.

Laylo started in 2021 when two people spent a summer driving between Raglan and Kaikōura trying to find surf clothing that didn't feel like it was designed by a committee in California.

They didn't find it. So they made it themselves. First drop was 80 tees out of a storage unit in Hamilton. They sold in four days.

We're still small on purpose. Limited drops. No middlemen. When something sells out, it stays out until we can make more properly.
