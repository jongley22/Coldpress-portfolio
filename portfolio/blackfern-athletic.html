— Our Story

Made in NZ.
For NZ.

Blackfern started on a trail run outside Nelson in 2021. Two friends, one too-cold jacket, and the observation that everything on the market was built for someone else's mountains.

We make performance apparel for the way New Zealanders actually move — in environments that go from 35°C to 5°C in a single afternoon, on trails that aren't on any map, with no support crew and no backup plan.

Everything we make is tested here. Most of it is made here. All of it is designed here.
