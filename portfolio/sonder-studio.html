How we
work.

01
Listen First

Before a single sketch, we spend time understanding how you actually live. Not how you think you should live. Not what you've seen on Pinterest. How you move through a room, what matters to you at 7am versus 7pm.

02
Concept & Develop

A single cohesive concept. We don't give you three options to choose from — we give you one direction that's right, and we explain exactly why. Then we develop it until it's ready to build.

03
Build & Oversee

We manage the build. We're on site. We care whether the gap between the shelf and the wall is 3mm or 8mm. You shouldn't have to.

Sonder Studio, Christchurch

Two people.
One studio.

Sonder is a small studio by design. We take on a limited number of projects each year so we can give every one our full attention. No junior designers. No account managers. You work with us directly, from the first call to the final installation.

We started Sonder because we believed interior design had become too much about performance and not enough about people. Every project we take on is one we'd be proud to live in ourselves.

Clara ReidPrincipal Designer
